Who We Are

Advocating for dignity in every community

Ramona Foundation advocates for women's empowerment, supports the elderly, promotes education, and fosters sports development among youth — creating positive change from the ground up.

Our Mission

To uplift communities by providing support, education, and resources that promote self-reliance, dignity, and positive change across Malawi.

Our Vision

A society where every individual in Malawi has access to opportunities, care, and a hopeful future — regardless of background or circumstances.

What We Do

Our Programs

Each program is developed with local input and tailored to create maximum, lasting impact in the communities we serve.

Community Outreach

Supporting vulnerable families with essential supplies, counseling, and crisis intervention services.

Education Assistance

Providing scholarships, school supplies, and infrastructure support to local schools across Malawi.

Youth Empowerment

Skills training, mentorship, and entrepreneurship programs designed for Malawi's next generation.

Health & Welfare

Health education, sanitation projects, and direct support for local healthcare facilities.
