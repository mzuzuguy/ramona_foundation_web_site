About Us

Ramona Foundation is an NGO dedicated to advocating for women's empowerment, supporting the elderly, promoting education, and fostering sports development among youth. The foundation aims to create positive change and uplift communities.

Our Mission

To uplift communities by providing support, education, and resources that promote self-reliance, dignity, and positive change.

Our Vision

A society where every individual in Malawi has access to opportunities, care, and a hopeful future, regardless of their background or circumstances.

Our Programs

We run several programs designed to address the most pressing needs in our communities. Each program is developed with local input and tailored to create maximum impact.

Community Outreach

Supporting vulnerable families with essential supplies, counseling, and crisis intervention.

Education Assistance

Providing scholarships, school supplies, and infrastructure support to local schools.

Youth Empowerment

Skills training, mentorship, and entrepreneurship programs for Malawi's youth.

Health & Welfare

Health education, sanitation projects, and support for healthcare facilities.
